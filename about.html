OUR STORY

Since 1982, OAK SHOE has been crafting premium leather footwear that combines timeless design with uncompromising quality.

Our Mission

At OAK SHOE, we believe that exceptional footwear begins with exceptional materials. Our mission is to create shoes that not only look beautiful but stand the test of time, using only the finest oak-bark tanned leathers and traditional shoemaking techniques.

Each pair is a testament to our commitment to quality, comfort, and sustainable craftsmanship.

Our Vision

We envision a world where consumers value craftsmanship over fast fashion, where shoes are cherished for decades rather than discarded after a season. OAK SHOE leads this movement by creating heirloom-quality footwear that develops character with age.

By 2030, we aim to source 100% of our materials from certified sustainable suppliers while maintaining our exacting standards.

The Craftsmanship

Every OAK SHOE is handcrafted by our master shoemakers in our Florence workshop. We employ techniques passed down through generations, combining them with modern innovations for unparalleled comfort.

From the selection of hides to the final polish, each of the 200+ steps in our process is executed with meticulous attention to detail.

Heritage

Founded by Antonio Rossi in a small workshop in Tuscany, OAK SHOE began as a supplier to local nobility. Our signature oak-bark tanning process, developed in 1985, gives our leathers their distinctive rich color and durability.

While we've grown from those humble beginnings, we remain true to our roots - every pair still bears Antonio's original mark as a seal of quality.
